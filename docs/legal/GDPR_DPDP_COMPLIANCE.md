## 🇪🇺 **GDPR Compliance (EU Users)**

### **1. Legal Basis for Processing (Article 6)**

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

| Processing Activity | Legal Basis | GDPR Article |
|---------------------|-------------|--------------|
| Account creation & authentication | Consent, Contract | 6(1)(a), 6(1)(b) |
| Service provision (research, AI analysis) | Contract, Legitimate Interest | 6(1)(b), 6(1)(f) |
| Platform improvement & analytics | Legitimate Interest | 6(1)(f) |
| Marketing communications | Consent | 6(1)(a) |
| Legal compliance | Legal Obligation | 6(1)(c) |

### **2. Data Subject Rights (Chapter 3)**

We fully support all GDPR rights:

#### **✅ Right to Access (Article 15)**
- **What:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **How:** Email [dpo@junior-legal.com]
- **Timeline:** Within 1 month (extendable by 2 months if complex)
- **Format:** Machine-readable JSON or PDF

#### **✅ Right to Rectification (Article 16)**
- **What:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **How:** Account settings or contact DPO
- **Timeline:** Without undue delay

#### **✅ Right to Erasure/"Right to be Forgotten" (Article 17)**
- **What:** Delete your personal data
- **How:** Account deletion or email request
- **Timeline:** Within 30 days
- **Exceptions:** Legal obligations, public interest

#### **✅ Right to Restriction of Processing (Article 18)**
- **What:** Limit how we process your data
- **How:** Contact DPO with reason
- **Timeline:** Immediate upon verification

#### **✅ Right to Data Portability (Article 20)**
- **What:** Receive data in machine-readable format
- **How:** Export feature in account settings
- **Format:** JSON, CSV
- **Includes:** Profile, documents, search history

#### **✅ Right to Object (Article 21)**
- **What:** Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- **How:** Opt-out settings or contact DPO
- **Effect:** We will cease processing unless compelling grounds exist

#### **✅ Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7)**
- **What:** Revoke permission at any time
- **How:** Account settings or email
- **Effect:** Processing stops (does not affect lawfulness of past processing)

#### **✅ Right to Lodge a Complaint (Article 77)**
- **Where:** Supervisory authority in your EU member state
- **Alternative:** Contact our DPO first for resolution

## 🇮🇳 **DPDP Act 2023 Compliance (Indian Users)**

### **9. Data Fiduciary Obligations (Section 8)**

Junior acts as a **Data Fiduciary** under the DPDP Act.

**Our Duties:**

✅ **Purpose Limitation:** Process data only for specified purposes  
✅ **Data Minimization:** Collect only necessary data  
✅ **Accuracy:** Keep data accurate and up-to-date  
✅ **Storage Limitation:** Retain data only as long as needed  
✅ **Security:** Implement reasonable safeguards  
✅ **Transparency:** Provide clear privacy notices

### **10. Consent Management (Section 6)**

**10.1 How We Obtain Consent**

- ✅ **Clear & Specific:** Separate consent for each purpose
- ✅ **Informed:** Explain what data, why, and how
- ✅ **Free:** No bundling of services with consent
- ✅ **Unconditional:** Can refuse without penalty
- ✅ **Affirmative Action:** Explicit opt-in required

**10.2 Consent Dashboard**

Accessible at: Account Settings → Privacy & Consent

Manage:
- Marketing communications
- Analytics tracking
- AI model training (anonymized)
- Third-party data sharing

### **11. Rights of Data Principals (Section 11-14)**

#### **✅ Right to Access (Section 11)**
